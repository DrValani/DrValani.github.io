A closer look at the zero's pinching the real axis

On this page we take a closer look at the "pinch" of the two outer arms of zeros on the real axis. A plot of the anti-ferromagnetic zeros of a 5x5x10 lattice with q=2 is shown below. The parametric graph f(x(t), y(t)) = a*cos(t)(1+cos(t+k)) + b, c*sin(t)(1+cos(t+k))+d is also plotted. Where a, b, c, d and k are real value constants.
